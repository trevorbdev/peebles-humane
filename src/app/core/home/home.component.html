Welcome to Peebles Humane Society!

We are a non-profit humane society that specializes in finding your new best friend! Whether you are looking for a cat, dog, bird, rabbit, etc., we have a mission to cater as much to our pets as we do potential owners. We take the due dillegence to make sure that our pets are going to the best place possible.
